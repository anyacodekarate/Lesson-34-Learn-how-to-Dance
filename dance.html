Indian Classical Dance Forms

India is known for its rich and diverse culture, and its classical dance forms are an integral part of its heritage.

Bharatanatyam

A classical dance form from Tamil Nadu, known for its grace and storytelling through gestures.

Kathak

A North Indian classical dance that focuses on intricate footwork and storytelling.

Odissi

A classical dance form from Odisha, known for its fluid movements and sculpturesque postures.

Modern Dance Forms

Modern dance is a broad genre that encompasses various styles, all characterized by free and expressive movement.

Hip-Hop

A street dance style that evolved alongside hip-hop music, known for its upbeat and dynamic movements.

Contemporary

A fusion of classical and modern techniques that emphasizes expression and emotion.

Jazz

A lively dance form rooted in African-American culture, filled with rhythm, energy, and personality.
